About Us

We are a dedicated team of environmental scientists, data analysts, and activists committed to solving the global plastic waste crisis. Our mission is to leverage technology and data to create a cleaner, more sustainable future for all.

Founded in 2023, PlasticAwareness.org has grown into a global force for change, with projects spanning from local community cleanups to international policy advocacy.

Our Solutions

We tackle plastic waste through a multi-faceted approach:

Advanced Recycling

Developing and promoting new technologies to recycle previously unrecyclable plastics.

Circular Economy

Working with businesses to design products for longevity, reuse, and recyclability.

Policy & Advocacy

Pushing for stronger regulations on plastic production and waste management.

Public Awareness

Educating communities about the impact of plastic and how they can help.
